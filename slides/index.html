Submit Your Certification

Learn how to download your uCertify Certificates (for ICT) and share them via OneDrive.

Step 1:

Accessing Your Certificate

  1. Login to uCertify.
  2. Click on the bookcase icon labeled "My Library".
  3. Navigate to the course you have completed (make sure it is a "blue subject") and open it.
  4. Click the light-blue button labeled "Share and Print My Certificate".
Step 2:

Downloading Your Certificate

  1. Click the (PDF) icon.
